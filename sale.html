RELATIONSHIPS ARE OUR FOUNDATION.

Café Integral is more than a simple coffee importer and roasting company. As an entirely vertical operation, we believe excellence at every stage of our coffee's lifecycle is fundamental to our success.

Throughout our relationships with our coffee-producing partners, we seek to achieve an ideal balance of research, education, and support. We strive to usher small, quality-driven Nicaraguan growers towards higher standards of production and sustainability.

Founded in New York, Café Integral focuses on working with like-minded companies to help express their unique vision. Through our experience, we understand what it takes to run a successful coffee operation and believe it is essential to recognize the variables and pressures of the market to remain responsive and dynamic.

To work with us is to participate in brewing a brighter future for everyone.
